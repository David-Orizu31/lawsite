About Lawsite


Lawsite is one of Sweden's leading business law law firms. Our roots go all the way back to the 19th century, but the modern Lawsite we are today was founded in 2002.


Since then, it has gone away. We have grown rapidly to today be one of the largest law firms in Sweden, with offices in Gothenburg, Malmö, Stockholm and Sundsvall. Our starting point is to deliver legal services with the highest quality and believe that this is done in the best way through a modern organization and a proactive thinking.
